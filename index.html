Products We Offer

We provide a comprehensive range of high-performance transformers designed for efficiency, durability, and compliance with the latest industrial standards.

⚡ Distribution Transformers

The backbone of your manufacturing line.

  • Capacity: 16 kVA to 2500 kVA
  • Certification: BIS Energy Efficiency Level II.
  • Applications: Residential complexes, small industries, and rural electrification.
  • Feature: Low loss, high durability, and low maintenance.

🔌 Power Transformers

For heavy-duty industrial and utility applications.

  • Capacity: High-performance units up to 5000 kVA.
  • Voltage: Robust designs for up to 33 kV Class.
  • Applications: Large manufacturing plants, substations, and solar parks.
  • Feature: Robust design for continuous high-load operations.

🏢 Dry-Type Transformers

The safe choice for indoor and high-occupancy areas.

  • Insulation: Vacuum Pressure Impregnated (VPI)
  • Fire Safety: Self-extinguishing and moisture-resistant
  • Applications: Hospitals, shopping malls, high-rise buildings, and IT parks.
  • Feature: No oil leak risk, eco-friendly, and minimal clearance required.

🛠 Servicing & Repair

Highlighting your expertise in life-extension.

  • Services: Complete rewinding, core repairing, and gasket replacement.
  • Maintenance: On-site Oil Filtration (BDV testing) and painting.
  • Testing: Rigorous post-repair testing to original manufacturer standards.
  • Expertise: Over 25 years of experience handling all major brands.
